Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Rewarded Runner ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use the App:

  • Account information: username and email address when you register.
  • Usage data: number of ads watched, points earned, and game activity.
  • Device information: device type, operating system version, and advertising ID (collected by Google AdMob).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Operate and maintain your account and points balance.
  • Display rewarded advertisements through Google AdMob.
  • Calculate estimated monthly gift amounts based on your points.
  • Improve the App experience.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

This App uses Google AdMob to display rewarded video ads. AdMob may collect and process data including device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. Google's privacy policy applies to AdMob data collection: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting: https://www.google.com/settings/ads.

4. Points and Rewards

The App awards points for watching rewarded ads. Points are tracked on our backend server. Points are not real currency. Any estimated gift amounts shown in the App are calculated estimates only. The App operator is solely responsible for any voluntary distributions. We make no guarantee of payment or reward.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your account data (username, email, points) is stored on a secure server. Passwords are hashed using industry-standard bcrypt encryption. Data is transmitted over HTTPS. We take reasonable steps to protect your data, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and data by contacting us at the email below.

7. Children's Privacy

This App is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Third-Party Services

Delete Your Account

If you would like to delete your Rewarded Runner account and all associated data, follow the steps below.

How to request account deletion:
  1. Send an email to support@rewardedrunner.com
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request
  3. Include the email address associated with your account
  4. We will process your request within 7 business days

What data will be deleted

  • Your username and email address
  • Your points balance and ad watch history
  • All account activity and game data

What data may be retained

Aggregated, anonymized analytics data (not linked to your identity) may be retained for up to 90 days for service improvement purposes, after which it is permanently deleted.

For any questions about your data or this process, contact us at:
